Listings

A listing is a public advertisement to show your interest in either buying or selling an Old School RuneScape name. They can be classified into categories to help group together similar themed names, as well as making searching for a general themed name easier.

Reputation

Your reputation helps guide the community to how trusted you are. The people you interact with can will rate your performance as a customer or seller. Maintain a professional image, gain a positive rating, and increase the chance of a sale.

Store

A store is unique page unlocked on the highest tier of account. It publicly shows the positive ratings left by customer, as well as the users current listings. Take your business to the next level and increase sales with your own store.
